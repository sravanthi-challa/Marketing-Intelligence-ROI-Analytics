# Stakeholder Analysis

## Project Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Role | Business Needs | Key Decisions |
|-------------|------|----------------|---------------|
| CEO | Executive Leadership | Overall marketing performance | Approve marketing strategy and investment |
| Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) | Marketing Head | Campaign ROI, ROAS, channel performance | Allocate marketing budget |
| Marketing Manager | Campaign Management | Campaign-level insights | Optimize or pause campaigns |
| Finance Manager | Financial Oversight | Marketing spend, profitability | Control marketing expenses |
| Regional Manager | Regional Operations | Regional campaign performance | Improve regional marketing strategy |
| Data Analyst | Analytics Team | Reliable data and KPIs | Generate insights and recommendations |

---

## Stakeholder Expectations

### CEO

Needs a high-level dashboard showing:

- Total Revenue
- Total Marketing Spend
- Overall ROI
- Overall Profit
- Revenue Trend

---

### Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

Needs:

- Campaign comparison
- Channel comparison
- Budget utilization
- ROI analysis
- Customer acquisition metrics

---

### Marketing Manager

Needs:

- Best-performing campaigns
- Worst-performing campaigns
- Conversion rates
- Campaign optimization opportunities

---

### Finance Manager

Needs:

- Marketing costs
- Profitability
- Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

---

### Regional Manager

Needs:

- Regional performance
- Customer demographics
- Regional conversion trends

---

### Data Analyst

Responsible for:

- SQL analysis
- KPI calculations
- Power BI dashboards
- Business recommendations
