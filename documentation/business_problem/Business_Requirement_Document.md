# Business Requirement Document (BRD)

# Marketing Intelligence & ROI Analytics Platform

## 1. Project Overview

### Background

ShopSphere is a fictional global e-commerce company that invests heavily in digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels, including Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Email Marketing, and Affiliate Marketing.

The company wants to evaluate campaign performance, optimize marketing spend, improve customer acquisition, and maximize return on investment (ROI). However, the marketing team currently lacks a centralized analytics platform that provides a complete view of campaign effectiveness.

This project aims to design an end-to-end Marketing Intelligence & ROI Analytics Platform using MySQL, SQL, and Power BI to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

The company faces the following business challenges:

- Marketing budget is not optimally allocated.
- Some campaigns generate high costs with low returns.
- Performance varies significantly across marketing channels.
- Customer behavior differs by region, device, and demographic segment.
- Executives lack a unified dashboard to monitor marketing performance.
- Marketing decisions rely on fragmented reports instead of centralized analytics.

---

## 3. Project Goal

Develop a complete analytics solution that enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor campaign performance.
- Measure marketing profitability.
- Compare marketing channels.
- Analyze customer acquisition.
- Track key performance indicators (KPIs).
- Support strategic marketing decisions using interactive dashboards.

---

## 4. Business Objectives

- Improve Return on Investment (ROI).
- Improve Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).
- Reduce Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC).
- Increase Conversion Rate.
- Optimize Marketing Budget Allocation.
- Identify high-performing campaigns.
- Identify underperforming campaigns.
- Provide executive-level reporting through Power BI dashboards.

---

## 5. Expected Deliverables

- MySQL Database
- Optimized SQL Queries
- Professional Power BI Dashboard
- Business Insights
- Executive Recommendations
- GitHub Repository with Documentation

---

## 6. Project Scope

### Included

- Marketing campaign performance analysis
- Customer segmentation analysis
- Marketing channel comparison
- KPI engineering
- SQL-based business analysis
- Interactive Power BI dashboards
- Executive business recommendations

### Excluded

- Machine Learning models
- Real-time streaming analytics
- Cloud deployment
- API integration

---

## 7. Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it:

- Answers key business questions through SQL and Power BI.
- Provides actionable business recommendations.
- Demonstrates professional data modeling.
- Includes clear documentation.
- Is suitable for showcasing in a professional portfolio and discussing during interviews.
